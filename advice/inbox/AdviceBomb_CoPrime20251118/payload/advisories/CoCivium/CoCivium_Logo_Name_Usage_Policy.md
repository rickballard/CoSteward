# CoCivium Logo and Name Usage Policy

Effective: on publication

Allowed
- Descriptive, truthful references to the CoCivium name in news, research, and links.
- Use of our logo only under written permission or under a license that we publish.

Not allowed
- Any use that suggests partnership, endorsement, or affiliation without an official signed and logged claim.
- Altering the logo or using confusingly similar marks.
- Using the "Verified" or "Official" badge or lockups.

Verification rule
- Only assets that are digitally signed and present in our public transparency log are official.
- Each official asset carries a fingerprint and a link or QR to a verify page.

Enforcement
- We may revoke permission at any time.
- We seek removals and legal remedies for misuse, fraud, or impersonation.
- Report misuse: provide URLs, screenshots, and contact details to our report form.
