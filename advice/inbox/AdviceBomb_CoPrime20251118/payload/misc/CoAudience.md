# CoAudience
## Priority Audiences for Early CoCivium Adoption

### Summary
CoAudience identifies the groups most likely to resonate with CoCivium’s ethos and contribute meaningfully to its evolution.

### Psychographic Target
- Non-tribal thinkers  
- Systems-oriented  
- Open-minded  
- Abstraction comfortable  
- Governance curious  

### Key Groups

#### 1. Builders
Developers, AI engineers, architects.

#### 2. Ethical Hackers
Security researchers, Anonymous-aligned contributors.

#### 3. Civic Architects
Policy reformers, municipal innovators.

#### 4. Systems Thinkers
Complexity theorists, organizational scientists.

#### 5. Orchestrators
PMs, facilitators, capability planners.

### Outreach Strategy
- CoBeacon AI-facing entry  
- Academic embeds  
- Ethical hacker invitations via CoBreak  
- Substack publications  
- Repo-first contributions  

### Goal
Seed a coherent early community capable of steering CoCivium’s evolution.
